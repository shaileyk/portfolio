AI Payslip Analyzer

A fully local, privacy-first payslip verification tool for NBFCs and lending teams. Uses on-device AI vision (Gemma 4 via Ollama) and OCR to automate income verification — no data leaves the device, zero cloud API calls.

Problem: Credit analysts manually review payslips for income accuracy and statutory validity, slowing loan TAT and introducing human error.

My AI Product Philosophy

Privacy by Architecture

Sensitive financial data should never leave the device. Local-first AI (Ollama, on-device models) makes privacy structural — not a policy, a guarantee.

AI + Rules = Trust

LLMs are powerful but probabilistic. The best financial AI pairs model output with deterministic rule checks — statutory math, legal thresholds, format validation — to produce auditable, explainable results.

PM Who Ships

I build working prototypes before writing PRDs. Getting hands-on with the AI stack — prompts, confidence scores, edge cases — produces better product decisions than requirements written from a distance.

Growth is the Product

The best fintech products don't just work — they change behaviour. I obsess over metrics that matter: repayment rates, engagement depth, retention, and revenue. Features are hypotheses; outcomes are the measure.
