Featured · AI · Fintech
● Live Prototype

AI Payslip Analyzer

A fully local, privacy-first payslip verification tool for NBFCs and lending teams. Detects tampered payslips using a combination of on-device AI vision (Gemma 4 via Ollama), OCR (Tesseract.js), and rule-based statutory checks — no cloud API, no data leaving the device.

Problem: Credit analysts waste hours manually checking payslips for PF/PT compliance violations and numerical tampering. Fraud slips through.
Solution: 11-point statutory check engine covering Indian tax law across 21 states. Confidence score 0–100 flags borderline cases for human review.
AI Stack: Gemma 4 vision model → Tesseract OCR fallback → regex rule engine. Priority-ordered for accuracy.

RBI Compliance Intelligence Platform

A local full-stack web app that automatically scrapes RBI circulars, uses Claude AI to extract actionable compliance items, and surfaces them in a dashboard for review and status tracking — turning dense regulatory PDFs into a clear, prioritized to-do list.

Problem: Compliance teams manually wade through dozens of RBI circulars per quarter, often missing action deadlines buried in dense regulatory language.

My AI Product Philosophy

Privacy by Architecture

Sensitive financial data should never leave the device. Local-first AI (Ollama, on-device models) makes privacy structural — not a policy, a guarantee.

AI + Rules = Trust

LLMs are powerful but probabilistic. The best financial AI pairs model output with deterministic rule checks — statutory math, legal thresholds, format validation — to produce auditable, explainable results.

PM Who Ships

I build working prototypes before writing PRDs. Getting hands-on with the AI stack — prompts, confidence scores, edge cases — produces better product decisions than requirements written from a distance.

Compliance as a Feature

In fintech, regulatory requirements aren't friction — they're the product. The best tools make compliance invisible to the analyst while satisfying the regulator.
